Samsung Galaxy

Neither the Samsung Galaxy M31 has acquired it from its predecessor - the Galaxy M30. At first glance, it is quite difficult to distinguish between the two devices. The smartphone comes with a 6.4-inch Infinity U display, surrounded by thin bezels all around. The smartphone features smooth edges and rounded corners, which makes the device comfortable to hold.Apart from the larger display, the front also has a 32MP selfie camera. On the right side are the volume rocker and power button and on the left is the SIM card tray and microSD card slot. The bottom of the device has a mono speaker, USB Type-C charging port and 3.5mm audio jack. The Samsung Galaxy M31 comes with a shiny rear panel that attracts a lot of fingerprints. The back panel has a quad-camera setup, fingerprint sensor and Samsung branding. In the design section, the smartphone does not fail to impress.
